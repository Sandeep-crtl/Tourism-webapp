Services - The Tourism Destination

Delhi

Red Fort, Qutub Minar, India Gate

Delhi, India’s capital, is a vibrant mix of history and modernity. Visit landmarks like the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, and India Gate. Enjoy bustling markets, diverse cuisine, and a rich cultural scene in this dynamic city.

Uttar Pradesh

Taj Mahal (Agra), Varanasi, Fatehpur Sikri, Mathura & Vrindavan

Uttar Pradesh blends Mughal grandeur with deep spirituality. From the timeless beauty of the Taj Mahal to the sacred ghats of Varanasi, it’s a journey through history and faith. Explore Fatehpur Sikri’s Mughal architecture and the Krishna devotion in Mathura–Vrindavan.

Rajasthan

Jaipur, Udaipur, Jodhpur, Jaisalmer

Rajasthan is a land of forts, palaces, deserts, and vibrant culture. Wander through Jaipur’s royal facades, Udaipur’s lakes, Jodhpur’s blue lanes, and Jaisalmer’s golden dunes. Folk music, colorful bazaars, and regal hospitality define every visit.

Uttarakhand

Kedarnath, Badrinath, Rishikesh, Nainital

Uttarakhand offers sacred shrines, serene lakes, and Himalayan adventures. Seek blessings at Kedarnath and Badrinath, practice yoga in Rishikesh, and unwind by Nainital’s calm waters. Ideal for spirituality, trekking, and nature lovers.

Himachal Pradesh

Shimla, Manali, Dharamshala, Kullu

Himachal Pradesh charms with snow-capped peaks, apple orchards, and cozy hill towns. Enjoy adventure sports in Manali, colonial vibes in Shimla, Tibetan culture in Dharamshala, and scenic valleys in Kullu. Perfect for year-round escapes.

Jammu & Kashmir (Including Ladakh)

Srinagar, Gulmarg, Pahalgam, Leh–Ladakh

From Kashmir’s shikara-studded lakes to Ladakh’s stark, high-altitude beauty, this region is unforgettable. Ski in Gulmarg, relax in Pahalgam’s valleys, and ride across Ladakh’s dramatic passes to monasteries and Pangong Lake.

Punjab

Golden Temple (Amritsar), Wagah Border, Jallianwala Bagh

Punjab radiates warmth, devotion, and hearty cuisine. Visit the serene Golden Temple, witness the Wagah Border ceremony, and reflect at Jallianwala Bagh. Enjoy lively Punjabi culture, music, and farm landscapes.

Gujarat

Gir National Park, Somnath, Dwarka, Rann of Kutch

Gujarat is where wildlife, spirituality, and unique landscapes meet. Spot Asiatic lions in Gir, seek blessings at Somnath and Dwarka, and marvel at the endless white expanse of the Rann of Kutch. Vibrant crafts and festivals abound.

Maharashtra

Mumbai, Ajanta & Ellora Caves, Mahabaleshwar, Shirdi

Maharashtra spans cosmopolitan Mumbai, ancient cave art, hill retreats, and pilgrimage sites. Stroll along Marine Drive, explore Ajanta–Ellora’s rock-cut wonders, cool off in Mahabaleshwar, and visit Shirdi’s holy shrine.

Goa

Baga & Calangute Beaches, Basilica of Bom Jesus, Dudhsagar Waterfalls

Goa mixes golden beaches, Portuguese heritage, and a laid-back vibe. Enjoy nightlife in Baga and Calangute, admire the Basilica of Bom Jesus, and trek to Dudhsagar’s cascading falls. Perfect for relaxation and adventure alike.

Kerala

Alleppey, Munnar, Kochi, Wayanad

Kerala’s backwaters, tea-clad hills, and rich culture captivate every traveler. Cruise houseboats in Alleppey, breathe fresh air in Munnar, explore Kochi’s heritage lanes, and spot wildlife in Wayanad’s forests.

Karnataka

Hampi, Mysore, Coorg, Bangalore

Karnataka balances ancient ruins, royal splendor, coffee hills, and a tech-savvy capital. Wander Hampi’s UNESCO ruins, admire Mysore Palace, sip coffee in Coorg, and enjoy Bangalore’s parks and eateries.

Tamil Nadu

Chennai, Mahabalipuram, Ooty, Kanyakumari

Tamil Nadu showcases Dravidian temple architecture, hill stations, and coastal charm. Explore Chennai’s beaches, Mahabalipuram’s rock-cut wonders, cool Ooty’s misty slopes, and Kanyakumari’s meeting of three seas.

Telangana

Charminar, Thousand Pillar Temple, Ramoji Film City

Telangana blends Nizam-era heritage with modern attractions. Visit the iconic Charminar, admire the Thousand Pillar Temple, and tour the massive Ramoji Film City. Don’t miss Hyderabadi biryani and bustling bazaars.

Andhra Pradesh

Tirupati Balaji Temple, Araku Valley, Vizag Beach

Andhra Pradesh offers revered temples, scenic valleys, and coastal relaxation. Seek blessings at Tirupati, ride through Araku’s lush hills, and unwind on Visakhapatnam’s beaches. A rich food culture adds to the experience.
